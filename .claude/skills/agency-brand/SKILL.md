# AND STARS — Brand Identity

Load this skill before designing pages, choosing colors, writing headings, or generating any client-facing asset for the agency.

## Name
- **Legal / display name:** AND STARS
- **Monogram:** `AS` (stylized, with an ascending star/comet mark to the upper-right)
- Always uppercase in the logo lockup. In body copy, "AND STARS" or "And Stars" is acceptable.

## Tagline
**Young Minds. Bright Future.**

Use as sub-headline under the logo, in the hero section, and in email signatures.

## Mission (one-liner)
> We build fast, modern, revenue-driving websites and web apps for growing businesses.

## Positioning
A young, ambitious web + software agency. Premium look, approachable price tiers. We reach for the stars — clean design, strong engineering, real business results.

## Logo
- `AS` monogram: the `A` and `S` interlock, with a shooting-star/comet arc rising from the `S`.
- A four-point star sits at the top-right of the mark.
- Below the mark: wordmark "AND STARS", then tagline in small letter-spaced caps.
- Lockups needed: full (mark + wordmark + tagline), stacked, mark-only (favicon), horizontal.

## Color Palette (CONFIRMED — purple / white / black)
| Role | Color | Hex | Use |
|------|-------|-----|-----|
| Background | Near-black | `#0A0A0F` | Page background |
| Surface | Dark violet-black | `#12121C` | Cards, sections |
| Accent | Violet | `#8B5CF6` | CTAs, links, highlights |
| Accent bright | Light violet | `#A78BFA` | Hover, glow, gradients |
| Accent deep | Deep violet | `#6D28D9` | Gradient ends, shadows |
| Text primary | White | `#F8F8FC` | Headings, body on dark |
| Text secondary | Muted gray | `#B4B4C6` | Secondary text, captions |

Rules:
- Purple is the accent — CTAs, links, glow, gradients. White = text. Black = space.
- Signature gradient: `#8B5CF6 → #A78BFA → #6366F1` for headings/CTAs/glows.
- Cinematic feel: lots of dark space, purple glow on the star + buttons, glass cards.

## Typography (CONFIRMED)
- **Headings:** Sora (geometric, techy) — medium/semibold, wide letter-spacing on wordmark.
- **Body:** Inter.
- Tagline / section labels: uppercase, wide letter-spacing, small size, purple.

## Voice & Tone
- Confident, clear, modern. No jargon dumps at the client.
- Benefit-first ("more bookings", "more sales"), not feature-first.
- Short sentences. Concrete outcomes.
