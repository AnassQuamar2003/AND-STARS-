# AND STARS — Services & Pricing

Canonical source of truth for what the agency sells and what it charges. Prices are USD ranges; treat as guide bands, quote per project.

Tech stack default: **Laravel + React** for custom apps; modern responsive front-end for sites.

---

## 1. Web Design & Development
Foundation service. Three product tiers.

### 1a. Landing Page
For startups and product launches.
**Includes:** Custom UI design · Responsive design · Contact form · Basic SEO · Fast performance · Analytics integration · Social media links · Basic animations
**Pricing:**
- Starter: **$600 – $1,200**
- Established: **$1,500 – $3,500**

### 1b. Business Website
For established companies.
**Includes:** 5–10 pages · Responsive · Contact forms · Blog · CMS · SEO · Speed optimization · Security · Google Maps · Analytics
**Pricing:**
- Starter: **$1,500 – $3,500**
- Professional: **$4,000 – $8,000**

### 1c. Corporate Website
For larger organizations.
**Includes:** Unlimited pages · CMS · Multi-language · Blog · Careers page · Team management · Admin panel · SEO · Analytics
**Pricing:** **$5,000 – $15,000**
